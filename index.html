- 4 Generations from father to son -

A family business since 1896, the two brothers, Patrick and Olivier, are the great grandsons of the founder Jules Bacquié and the fourth generation of roasters. We strive to guarantee exceptional quality in our coffees and teas as well as in our selection of food products and spirits.

The store offers the finest freshly roasted Arabicas, and tea enthusiasts can choose from over 180 varieties from Cannon, Cie Coloniale, Christine Dattner, Dammann, Kusmi Tea and Mariage Frères. We also have a delicatessen section, with traditional products such as foie gras, smoked salmon, caviar and regional products.

On the cellar side, there is a large display of whiskies, armagnacs, wines, champagnes and other spirits.
